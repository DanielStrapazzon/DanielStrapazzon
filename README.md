Olá! Me chamo **Daniel José Henrique Strapazzon**, estou cursando **Engenharia de Computação na UNOESC** e atuo como **Programador Trainee na ONIX Automação Industrial** — especializada na prestação de serviços e implementação de projetos para processos industriais.

## 🇺🇸 About Me

Hi! My name is **Daniel José Henrique Strapazzon**, I'm studying **Computer Engineering at UNOESC** and working as a **Trainee Programmer at ONIX Automação Industrial** — a company specialized in delivering services and implementing projects for industrial processes.

I also run the **Daniel Strapazzon | Dev** YouTube channel, where I share content about technology in the real world: programming, hardware, automation, embedded systems, and IoT.

My goal is to go beyond just writing code — to truly understand concepts, solution architecture, and the reasoning behind every implementation.
